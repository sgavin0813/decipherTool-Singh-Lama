Columnar Transposition Cipher

The assumption for columnar transposition is that when there are multiple occurrences of characters in the keyword, the columns corresponding to these repeated characters are arranged in the order they appear in the keyword. For example, in the keyword "ANALYSTA", it can be interpreted as "A1 N A2 LYST A3", and would be arranged as "A1 A2 A3 LNSTY".
